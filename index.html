Frequently Asked Questions

How do I directly get connected with customer connect?

You can reach out to us via contact number provided or email at healthscancare2024@gmail.com. We're available 24/7 to assist you!

What all services do we offer?

We offer a wide range of diagnostic services including full-body checkups, blood tests, sugar tests, dengue, malaria, thyroid tests, and more. We also provide free home collection services.

When will I get my report? And how will I come to know that my report is ready?

Reports are typically available within 24-48 hours after sample collection. You will be notified via SMS or email once your report is ready, and you can also check the status by contacting us.

Will I also get a hardcopy of my report?

Yes, you can request a hardcopy of your report at our center. Alternatively, we can dispatch it to your address for an additional fee.

Will there be additional charges to get report dispatched at my registered address?

Yes, there may be a small additional charge for dispatching the report to your address. Please contact us for the exact fee based on your location.

What are the different payment options?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and online bank transfers. You can choose the most convenient option at the time of booking.

Are there home collection charges separately for each member?

No, home collection charges are typically per visit, not per member. However, this may vary based on the number of tests and distance. Please confirm with us during booking.

Do I require to remain fasting?

Fasting requirements depend on the test. For example, Blood Glucose Fasting (FBS) requires fasting for 8-12 hours. We will inform you of any specific requirements when you book your test.
